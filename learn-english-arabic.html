Why Arabic Speakers Struggle with English

Arabic and English are fundamentally different. Arabic is written right to left, uses a root-based word system, does not have the verb "to be" in present tense, and has no articles like "a" or "the" in the same way. These differences cause specific recurring mistakes that standard English apps never address — because they were not designed for Arabic speakers.

NativeEnglish.fyi is different. Every grammar rule, every mistake correction, every phrase explanation is shown in Arabic alongside English. You understand the WHY — not just what to memorise.
