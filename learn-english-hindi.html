Why Hindi Speakers Struggle with English

Hindi and English have completely different structures. Hindi is a Subject-Object-Verb language — you say "मैंने खाना खाया" (I food ate) not "I ate food." English word order is fixed as Subject-Verb-Object. This causes hundreds of grammar mistakes that no standard English app explains — because they were not built for Hindi speakers.

NativeEnglish.fyi is different. Every rule, every mistake, every phrase is explained in Hindi alongside English. You understand WHY — not just what to say.
