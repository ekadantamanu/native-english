Why Telugu Speakers Struggle with English

Telugu and English are completely different in structure. Telugu is a Subject-Object-Verb language — you say "I rice ate" not "I ate rice." English is Subject-Verb-Object. This one difference causes hundreds of grammar mistakes that no standard English app explains — because they were not built for Telugu speakers.

NativeEnglish.fyi is different. Every explanation, every grammar rule, every mistake correction is shown in Telugu alongside English. You understand why — not just what.
